Usage Limits & Caveats

  • Session limits vary by message length, file attachments, conversation history, and model used.
  • 5-hour reset — your session usage allowance resets every 5 hours regardless of when your billing period started.
  • A weekly usage cap also applies across all models. Reset schedule is fixed, not tied to your subscription start date.
  • Anthropic may further limit usage (monthly caps, model restrictions) at their discretion.
  • Usage credits let you pay to continue past included limits. Credits are not part of the flat $20 fee — they are billed separately.
  • Pro is for individual use — not organizations. Teams plans start at $25/seat/month.
  • Priority access during peak traffic is included.
  • Early access to new features and models is included.
  • Prices and plans are subject to change at Anthropic's discretion.
  • Only available in supported regions.
